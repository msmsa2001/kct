ABOUT

Welcome to Khidmat Charitable Trust (KCT)

Khidmat Charitable Trust (KCT) is a Non-Government Organization (NGO) registered under the Government of India’s Public Trust Registration Act of 1950 on 12 December 2001. Based in Mumbai, KCT’s registration number is E19852.’

In 2001, by the grace of Almighty Allah, we established our start-up with the key objective of providing every possible medical aid to the poorer section of our society. Our aim is to provide them moral and economical support during the treatment of a critical illness. We also aim to provide support in the clearance of steep hospital and medicine bills.

In 2012 we came up with an education programme that provides educational help to the underprivileged students who are in need of such support to complete their education.

Later, in response to the overwhelming number of patients who started approaching us with dialysis issues, in 2016, we took the initiative of starting a Dialysis centre with our own team of doctors, nursing staff, consultants, and trustees. Today, we have a well-equipped, 16-bed Dialysis Centre at our own premises, where medical tests and other related expenses are also taken care of for the deserving patients. Last but not least, annual Ramzan aid and monthly rations is also a part of our programme for families in need.Over the years, KCT has managed to secure support from numerous philanthropists and charitable organizations from its many programmes.

Our Aim

To help underprivileged, deserving & needy people with their medical needs.

Our Vision

To deliver high quality services by designating concern for patient’s physical condition as well as their emotional needs. It is our desire to serve patients promptly with consideration and dignity.

Our Mission

To promote, restore, and maintain the health of all people.
