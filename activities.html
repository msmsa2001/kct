WHO BENEFITS FROM KCT ACTIVITIES?

Since our inception, Khidmat Charitable Trust is committed to playing the role of a catalyst in the provision, care and uplift of people living below the poverty line. This includes, not just those in need of critical disease management but also migrant, daily wagers, widows, orphans and other vulnerable sections of our society, dealing with challenging life situations. Religion is not a barrier. KCT stands committed to bring respect and self-sufficiency to every deserving human being, who needs our support.
